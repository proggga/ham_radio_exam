# Digital Transmission

## Speed Definitions
*   **Baud Rate (Bd)**: The symbol rate (number of signal changes per second).
*   **Bit Rate (bps)**: The rate of information transfer.
    *   $\text{Bit Rate} = \text{Baud Rate} \times \text{Bits per Symbol}$.
    *   If a symbol represents 1 bit (2 states), Baud = bps.
    *   If a symbol represents 4 bits (16 states), bps = 4 $\times$ Baud.

## Modulation Types
*   **ASK (Amplitude Shift Keying)**: Digital AM.
*   **FSK (Frequency Shift Keying)**: Switching between two tones (Mark/Space). Used in **RTTY**.
*   **PSK (Phase Shift Keying)**: Changing the phase of the carrier (BPSK, QPSK). Very efficient.
*   **QAM (Quadrature Amplitude Modulation)**: Combines Amplitude and Phase modulation for high data rates.
