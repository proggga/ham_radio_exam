# 20m Band (14 MHz)

The premier band for daytime Long-Haul DX.

## Characteristics
*   **Frequency:** 14.0 - 14.35 MHz.
*   **[[Solar Cycle & Propagation|Propagation]]:**
    *   **Day:** Excellent worldwide DX.
    *   **Night:** Stays open during Summer or Solar Max; closes in Winter/Solar Min.
*   **Skip Zone:** Always present. You generally cannot talk to locals (< 500km) on 20m.

## Usage
*   **Best For:** International DX, Contesting.
*   **Antennas:** Practical size (~33 ft dipole). [[Directional Antennas (Beams)|Beams]]/Yagis are common.
*   **Activity:** Highest of any band. Severe QRM during contests.
