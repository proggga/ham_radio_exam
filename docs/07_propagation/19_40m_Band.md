# 40m Band (7 MHz)

The most reliable all-around band in Amateur Radio.

## Characteristics
*   **Frequency:** 7.0 - 7.2/7.3 MHz.
*   **[Propagation](14_Solar_Cycle_&_Propagation.md):**
    *   **Day:** [NVIS](11_Near_Vertical_Incidence_Skywave_NVIS.md) (0-400 miles) to mid-range skip (300-900 miles).
    *   **Night:** Worldwide DX (F2 layer).
*   **Reliability:** Works well in **ALL** phases of the solar cycle.

## Usage
*   **Best For:** Everything. Local (Day), DX (Night), Contests.
*   **Challenges:**
    *   Extremely crowded.
    *   High broadcast interference (outside Region 2).
    *   Distinct "Skip Zone" usually develops in daytime.
