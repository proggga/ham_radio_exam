# The Ionosphere

This map covers the atmospheric layers that enable HF communication.

## Structure
*   **Ionospheric Layers** - D, E, F1, F2 layers.

## Dynamics
*   **Solar Cycle & Propagation** - Sunspots and Solar Flux.
*   **MUF, LUF, and Critical Frequency** - The frequency operating window.
*   **Fading (QSB)** - Multipath and Polarization fading.
