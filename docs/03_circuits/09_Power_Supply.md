# Power Supply

This map covers the conversion of mains power to useful DC for radio equipment.

## Stages
1.  **[Rectification](10_Rectification.md)** - AC to pulsating DC ([Diodes](../02_components/17_Diodes.md)).
2.  **[Power Supply Smoothing](11_Power_Supply_Smoothing.md)** - Pulsating DC to steady DC ([Capacitors](../02_components/05_Capacitors.md)/[Inductors](../02_components/09_Inductors_Spoelen.md)).
3.  **[Voltage Regulation](12_Voltage_Regulation.md)** - Stabilizing the voltage (Zener/Linear/IC).

## Types
*   **[Switch Mode Power Supply (SMPS)](13_Switch_Mode_Power_Supply_SMPS.md)** - High efficiency switching supplies ([SMPS](13_Switch_Mode_Power_Supply_SMPS.md)).
