# Station Status

Frequencies are allocated to services with a specific status.

## Primary Status (Primair)
*   **Rights**: Protected service.
*   **[Interference](../09_interference/03_Mixing_Products_Interference.md)**: Can claim protection from harmful interference from any other station.
*   **Must not cause**: Harmful interference to other Primary stations.

## Secondary Status (Secundair)
*   **Rights**: Lower priority.
*   **Must not cause**: Harmful interference to Primary stations.
*   **Cannot claim**: Protection from harmful interference from Primary stations.
*   **Can claim**: Protection from other Secondary stations (typically on a "first come, first served" basis).

## Co-Primary
When two services share a band with equal rights. Both must coordinate to avoid interference.
