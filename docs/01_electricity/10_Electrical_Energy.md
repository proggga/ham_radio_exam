# Electrical Energy (Arbeid/Energie)

[Energy](08_Power_and_Energy.md) is the capacity to do work, or the total power consumed over a period of time.

## Definition
*   **Symbol**: $W$ (Work) or $E$.
*   **Unit**: Joule ($J$) or Watt-second ($Ws$).

## Practical Units
In power distribution, the **Kilowatt-hour (kWh)** is used.
*   $1 \text{ kWh} = 1000 \text{ Watts} \times 3600 \text{ seconds} = 3,600,000 \text{ Joules}$.

## Formula
$$E = P \times t$$
*(Energy = Power $\times$ Time)*
