# Noise Types

[Noise](26_AC_Signals_&_Noise.md) (Ruis) sets the fundamental limit on receiver sensitivity.

## Thermal Noise
Generated by thermal agitation of electrons in any conductor or resistor.
*   **Formula**: $P_n = k \cdot T \cdot B$
    *   $k$ = Boltzmann's constant ($1.38 \times 10^{-23} J/K$).
    *   $T$ = Temperature (Kelvin).
    *   $B$ = [Bandwidth](../03_circuits/07_Bandwidth.md) (Hz).
*   **Implication**: Wider bandwidth = More noise. Cooling the receiver reduces noise.

## Shot Noise
Random fluctuations in current across semiconductor junctions (diodes/transistors).

## External Noise
*   **QRN**: Natural/Atmospheric noise (Static, Lightning). Dominant on [HF](../07_propagation/01_Propagation_Basics.md) bands.
*   **QRM**: Man-made interference.
*   **Galactic [Noise](26_AC_Signals_&_Noise.md)**: From the galaxy (dominant on [VHF](../07_propagation/07_VHFUHF_Bands_6m,_2m,_70cm.md)/[UHF](../07_propagation/07_VHFUHF_Bands_6m,_2m,_70cm.md) when looking at the sky).
