# Dutch HAM Radio Knowledge Gaps & Questions

This document lists specific topics where Dutch regulations (Agentschap Telecom / RDI) differ from the generic/UK syllabus. You need to find the answers to these questions to be fully prepared for the Netherlands exam (RZAM-F).

## 1. Licensing & Administrative (Eindterm 11 & 12)
- [ ] **License Classes:** What are the specific amateur radio license classes in the Netherlands? (e.g., Novice (N), Full (F))?
- [ ] **Call Sign Structure (Eindterm 11.1):**
  - What prefixes are assigned to which license class (PA, PB, PC, PD, PE, PH, PI)?
  - What is the structure (prefix, number, suffix)?
  - What are the rules for foreign amateurs in NL?
- [ ] **Specific Laws (Eindterm 12):**
  - **Telecommunicatiewet:** specifically definitions in Art 1.1 and handhaving in Art 15.3.
  - **Regeling gebruik frequentieruimte met meldingsplicht 2015:** What tables/articles apply to amateurs?
  - **CEPT T/R 61-01 & 61-02:** Distinction between temporary use vs permanent license (HAREC).

## 2. Band Plans & Frequency Privileges (Eindterm 11 & 12)
- [ ] **Frequency Allocations:**
  - What are the specific frequency limits for F license holders in NL?
  - **60m (5 MHz) Band:** Access rules and power limits?
  - **70 MHz (4m) Band:** Is this allowed in NL?
- [ ] **Power Limits:** What are the maximum power levels (PEP) permitted for Full license?

## 3. Technical & Safety Regulations (Eindterm 10 & 12)
- [ ] **EMF / Antennas (Eindterm 10.1):**
  - What are the permitted field strength limits (Watt/m) for human exposure?
  - Specific rules for antenna placement (omgevingsvergunning)?
- [ ] **Earthing Systems (Eindterm 10.2):**
  - Distinction between Phase, Neutral, Earth (colour codes).
  - Standard Dutch earthing practices (TT vs TN systems).
- [ ] **EMC Directive (Eindterm 12.4):**
  - Definition of electromagnetic compatibility (Art 3.1 lid 4).

## 4. Operating Procedures (Eindterm 11 & 13)
- [ ] **Codes:** Q-codes (standard list), but also any specific Dutch operational habits?
- [ ] **Emergency:** Procedures for disaster communication (DARES)?
- [ ] **Social:** "Goede relatie met de buren" (Good relationship with neighbors) - practical measures?

## 5. Authorities
- [ ] **RDI (Rijksinspectie Digitale Infrastructuur):** Role vs CBR.
- [ ] **Exam Institutes:** CBR (exam), RDI (license issuance).
